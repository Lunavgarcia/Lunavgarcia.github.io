Death: an opportunity to meet the creator

Muslims believe that Allah is in charge of their lives and decides how long they will live.

"Islamic Picture #029 Mobile & HQ resolution at: www.islamic.pictures/029" by IslamicPx is licensed under CC0 1.0

Islam teaches that there is life after death, and this is known as Akhirah. In Islam, most Muslims believe that when they die, they will stay in their graves until the Day of Judgement.

Those who have performed more good deeds than bad will enter Jannah, or Paradise. where there is no sickness, pain or sadness.

Those who have performed more bad deeds than good will enter Jahannam or Hell. This is a place of physical and spiritual suffering.
